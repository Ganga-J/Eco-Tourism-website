Environmental Impact

Our efforts are focused on three key areas: reforestation, marine conservation, and carbon offsetting. Each project is carefully selected to ensure maximum positive impact.

Reforestation Initiatives

Working with local partners, we have successfully planted over 12,500 native trees in a bid to restore deforested lands and provide new habitats for local wildlife. Our tree-planting programs are located in the Amazon and Sub-Saharan Africa.

Waste Management & Clean-Ups

Our travelers and volunteers contributed to multiple coastal and river clean-up projects, collecting over 5,800 pounds of plastic and other waste from vulnerable ecosystems.

Social & Community Impact

Sustainable tourism is a vehicle for social change. We are proud to have supported local economies and cultures by ensuring that our travel model benefits the communities we visit.

Economic Empowerment

We've created over 50 jobs for local guides and community leaders and invested $350,000+ directly into community-run businesses and initiatives. We believe that local control leads to more sustainable outcomes.

Cultural Preservation

Our programs include a strong educational component, designed to foster cross-cultural understanding and respect. We fund projects that help preserve indigenous languages and traditional knowledge.
