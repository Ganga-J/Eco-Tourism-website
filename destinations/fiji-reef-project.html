Dive into a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to help revive the stunning coral reefs of Fiji. Your adventure, your impact.

Your Impact on This Trip

Your participation directly supports:

  • Coral Restoration: The materials and expertise needed to cultivate and replant new coral colonies.
  • Marine Research: Data collection that helps our team monitor the health of the reef and plan future conservation efforts.
  • Local Education: Programs that teach local communities about sustainable fishing and marine conservation.

Coral Planting Excursions

Snorkel or dive to a designated reef to plant your own coral fragments and watch them take root.

Marine Biology Workshops

Attend daily sessions with marine biologists to learn about the threats to ocean health and how your work helps.
