Detailed Breakdown

Environmental Initiatives

We launched three new reforestation projects and expanded our partnership with the Global Coral Reef Alliance. This year, our focus was on critical ecosystems that are most vulnerable to climate change. Our data shows a 15% increase in native species in our key conservation areas, a direct result of our funding.

Community Empowerment

We believe economic sustainability is just as important as environmental sustainability. In 2024, our community partnerships resulted in:

  • $350,000+ invested directly into local, community-owned businesses.
  • 100% of our safari guides were hired and trained from the local Maasai community.
  • 5 new schools were supported with our educational grants.

Looking Forward: Our 2025 Goals

We are just getting started. In 2025, we aim to double our carbon offset commitment and launch two new programs focusing on freshwater conservation.
